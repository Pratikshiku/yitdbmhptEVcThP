## Dating Wedding Company Website Platform

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/64f4f7a5c5164e0ea786411903413d90/ghb20250919) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript`, etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any one of the listed software can be used to run)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Feature Description
The system is divided into (roles): regular users, administrators.

**Regular User Features:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Homepage
4. Wedding Companies
5. Wedding Cases
6. Blind Dating
7. Personal Center
8. Wedding Company Collection
9. Wedding Company Booking
10. Wedding Case Collection
11. Blind Dating Collection
12. Change Password
13. Personal Information
14. Wedding Company Information
15. Wedding Company Collection Management
16. Wedding Company Booking Management
17. Wedding Case Management
18. Wedding Case Collection Management
19. Blind Dating Management
20. Blind Dating Comment Management
21. Blind Dating Collection Management
22. Carousel Information

**Administrator Features:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center
3. Wedding Company Management
4. Wedding Company Collection Management
5. Wedding Company Booking Management
6. Wedding Case Management
7. Wedding Case Collection Management
8. User Management
9. Blind Dating Management
10. Blind Dating Comment Management
11. Blind Dating Collection Management
12. Carousel Management
